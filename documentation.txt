How to play:
Press '1' key on keyboard to start a game.
Press 'SPACE' to reset game.
Guess the missing number by pressing it on keyboard.
Player wins after guessing correctly 3 numbers consecutively.

Winning the game:
After player wins the time will be shown on top part of the screen. 
The time will also be saved to the table that shows last 3 times.

More information:
The previous 3 times are shown on the right part of the screen and sorted from newest to oldest.
The circle turns green when number has been guessed correctly and red when incorrectly.
